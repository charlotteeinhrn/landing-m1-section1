City

Sydney is the most populous city in Australia and Oceania, and the capital of the state of New South Wales (but not the capital of Australia, contrary to popular belief). It is located in the south-east of the country, on the shores of the Tasman Sea.

Sydney Opera House

One of the most famous buildings of the 20th century and a major venue for the arts, especially opera. Its original architecture, which looks like a sailboat to some, or a shell to others, was designed by the Dane Jorn Utzon.

Road in Australia

Through many mountain roads, you can discover the different landscapes of Australia.

The most famous animal in Australia: The Kangoroo

The kangaroo is a marsupial of the macropodid family typical of the Australian continent.

Royal National Park

Australia's Royal National Park lies immediately south of the metropolis of Sydney in New South Wales. It is Australia's first national park2 and the second oldest national park in the world.

Harbour Bridge

The Harbour Bridge is a metal arch bridge in the city of Sydney. It is the main crossing point across Sydney Harbour, allowing trains, cars and pedestrians to pass between the CBD and the north shore of the harbour. The view from the bridge and the nearby Opera House is iconic for the city and for Australia as a whole.
